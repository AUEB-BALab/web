_Georgios Alexopoulos_ is a doctoral candidate at the [Department of Business Administration](https://ba-en.uoa.gr/)
of the [University of Athens](https://en.uoa.gr/),
under the supervision of Associate Professor [Dimitris Mitropoulos](https://dimitro.gr/).
He also works as a Systems Architect at the [Greek National Infrastructures for Research and Technology (GRNET)](https://grnet.gr/en/).
Before joining the University of Athens,
and after finishing his undergraduate studies
in Electrical and Computer Engineering at [NTUA](https://www.ece.ntua.gr/en),
he was a Systems/Software Engineer at [Arrikto](https://www.arrikto.com/),
where he focused on ML systems.

His research lies at the intersection
of systems and software,
with a focus on improving
the security and reliability of computer systems.
He is currently scrutinizing the boundary between
scripting languages (e.g., Python, JavaScript)
and compiled languages (e.g., C/C++, Rust).
On the side, he is also trying to improve
the reliability of
Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) software.
