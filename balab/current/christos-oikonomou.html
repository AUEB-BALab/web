Christos - Oikonomou

Published: Wed 15 January 2014

In members.

Christos Oikonomou is a student at the Athens University of Economics and Business (AUEB), Department of Management Science and Technology.

In the summer of 2013, he helped in a research project regarding the evolution of prices at the Greek Ministry for Development and Competitiveness, under the supervision of Dr. Prodromidis, presented the results at the eleventh DMST Conference (13/05/2014); and tutored (TAed) certain microeconomic and macroeconomic review sessions for the freshmen class of 2013-14 at the request of Dr. Prodromidis. Also he has worked in extensive software systems under supervision of Dr. Diomidis Spinellis
