Markos - Gogoulos

Published: Fri 01 September 2006

In alumni.

Markos Gogoulos is a research associate of the SENSE group and a former member of the ISLAB group in N.C.S.R. Demokritos. He holds a Diploma in Information and Communication Systems Engineering from the University of the Aegean. His research interests include applied security and operating systems hardening, network attack analysis, operating systems and networking.
