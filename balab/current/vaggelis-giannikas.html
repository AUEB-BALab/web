Vaggelis - Giannikas

Published: Fri 04 September 2009

In members.

Vaggelis Giannikas holds a BSc in Management Science and Technology from the Athens University of Economics and Business (AUEB). He is currently a member of the SENSE group. His research interests include open source software, software engineering and supply chain information systems.

He also works at the European Project Unit of SingularLogic S.A. and collaborates with the ELTRUN, the e-business research center.
